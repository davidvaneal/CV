About

I am a biologist graduated from the University of Quindío in Colombia and a lover of insects, particularly dung beetles. I consider myself a passionate person about life and enjoy all activities related to it, such as research, which allows us to understand different natural processes and establish a connection with reality.

I love engaging in outdoor activities such as trekking, especially in high mountain areas, where people can reconnect with themselves and understand the true value of life.

“Sharing” is one of the key words for human development and therefore, I work daily to improve in this aspect and make a positive contribution to our natural and social environment.
